Hygiene Team Schedule — Hours Tally

Full day = 8 hrs  ·  Half day = 6 hrs  ·  Pay periods: May 27–Jun 9, Jun 10–Jun 23, every 2 weeks thereafter. May days prior to Jun 1 are not tracked.

How the rotation works: Full-timers (Tara, Liz, Carrie, Susan, Michelle) share one off slot every day Mon–Thu. On Tuesdays, part-timers (Lisa, Amber, Laurie) also rotate one off slot — making Tuesdays 2 off total. Fridays: Susan, Liz, and Carrie always work (fixed). Michelle, Laurie, Tara, Lisa, Amber, and Helen rotate equally for the remaining 3 slots — each sitting out roughly every other Friday. Helen works every Thursday and joins the Friday rotation. Doctor-out days pause the queue.
